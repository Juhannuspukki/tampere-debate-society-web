What is debating?

It's a bird species! Just kidding.

For many people, debating sounds like uncontrolled, unstructured yelling and aggressive arguing that leaves everyone annoyed.
Formal debating is something else!

It could be viewed as an academic sport aiming at the discussion of a controversial topic in a way that brings out many different points of view to the issue.

A key feature, which distinguishes formal debating from everyday quarreling is, that you will randomly draw a side which you will then represent. This will ensure, that it doesn't end up getting personal.

At the same time it offers the possibility for people to compete in a safe environment in the many aspects of debate.
This will help develope a variety of skills for debating, entering discussions and even help you speak during job interviews!

The skill of seeing many sides to one issue is of great importance, and one has to be able to question one’s own opinions – you never know when you might have to represent a completely different one!
